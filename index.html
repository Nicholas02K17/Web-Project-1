Fun Facts

The UEFA Champions League is one of the most prestigious club football tournaments in the world, featuring the top teams from across Europe.

The tournament began in 1955 as the European Cup. It was rebranded as the UEFA Champions League in 1992

Initially, it featured a knockout format; in 1992, it adopted a league phase, allowing for a group stage followed by knockout rounds

Teams qualify based on their performance in their national leagues and cups.
